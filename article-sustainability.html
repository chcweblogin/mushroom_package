Why Choose Us

Return to Earth: The Timeline of Decay

In the world of packaging, "disposable" is a dangerous myth. Traditional Styrofoam (EPS) doesn't typically go away; it breaks down into microplastics that persist in our oceans and food chains for centuries. Mushroom packaging offers a radical alternative: true circularity.

The 45-Day Promise

Our mycelium-based material is 100% home compostable. You don't need an industrial facility with high heat. You can simply break it up and toss it in your garden soil. Within 45 days, it will have completely returned to the earth, adding valuable nitrogen and nutrients back to the soil.

Feature Mycelium Packaging Styrofoam (EPS)
Decomposition Time 30-45 Days 500+ Years
Feedstock Agricultural Waste (Renewable) Petroleum (Fossil Fuels)
End of Life Soil Nutrient Microplastic Pollution
Toxicity Non-toxic Contains Styrene (Carcinogen)

A Carbon Negative Footprint

The sustainability story starts before the product is even finished. The growth process of mycelium actually sequesters carbon. By using agricultural waste that might otherwise be burned, and avoiding the energy-intensive extraction of fossil fuels, our production process has a fraction of the environmental impact of plastic foams.

Choosing mushroom packaging isn't just about reducing harm; it's about actively participating in a regenerative cycle. It’s packaging that protects your product today and protects the planet tomorrow.
