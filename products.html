How It's Made

Grown from the earth, returned to the earth.

1

The Mixture

We combine locally sourced hemp hurd (agricultural waste) with mushroom mycelium. This natural "glue" binds the hemp together.

2

Molding

The mixture is filled into custom molds. Unlike plastic, no heat or high pressure is needed—just biology doing its work.

3

Growth

Over 4-6 days, the mycelium grows through the hemp, creating a solid, durable shape that is white and velvety.

4

Baking

The product is heated to stop growth and remove moisture. The result is a sterile, strong, water-resistant package ready to ship.
